### Explanation 
Prompt engineering is the process of designing structured inputs (prompts) to obtain meaningful responses from AI systems. In this experiment, a real-world use case is selected, and different prompting techniques are applied to generate structured outputs.
## Selected Use Case – Online Food Delivery App: Placing and Tracking Orders
Background Information

* System: Online food delivery app

* Primary Actor: Customers placing and tracking orders

* Secondary Actor: Restaurant staff and delivery partners

* Goals: Enable smooth ordering, quick delivery, and real-time tracking

* Stakeholders: Restaurant owner, Delivery Manager, App Product Manager

* Preconditions: Customer registered on app; restaurant and delivery partners active

* Triggers: Customer selects food items and confirms the order
## Procedure:
 1.	Straightforward Prompts:
Definition: Direct prompts requesting specific information.
Examples:

    * Prompt: Define online food ordering in one sentence.
    * Output: Online food ordering allows customers to select, pay for, and request delivery of meals through a mobile or web app.
   * Prompt: List three benefits of using a food delivery app.
Output:
   * Convenient ordering from home
   * Real-time order tracking
   * Variety of restaurant options

Evaluation: Produces clear and direct responses for quick understanding.
  	
 2.	Tabular Format Prompting:

3. Preceding Question Prompting:
Definition: Follow-up questions for detailed explanation.

Examples:
* Prompt: Why is online food delivery popular? Explain its convenience for users.
    *  Output: Online food delivery is popular because it saves time, offers multiple restaurant choices, and allows users to order from anywhere without visiting the restaurant.

* Prompt: How do delivery tracking systems work? Describe their process.
   * Output: The app tracks the order in real-time by updating status at each stage—preparation, pickup, and delivery—ensuring transparency and timely delivery.

Evaluation: Provides detailed and explanatory responses.
   
5. Missing Word Prompting:
Definition: Uses fill-in-the-blank style prompts for precise answers.

Examples:
  *  Prompt: The primary actor placing orders is the ____.
          Output: Customer
  * Prompt: Orders are delivered by the ____ partner.
          Output: Delivery

Evaluation: Generates specific answers and tests knowledge effectively.
